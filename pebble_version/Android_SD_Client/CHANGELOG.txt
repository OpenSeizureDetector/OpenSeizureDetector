Android_SD_CLIENT CHANGELOG
===========================

V1.2   14 March 2015
       Added a 'Cancel Audible' button to the main screen to disable audible
       alarms for a short period (default 5 mins).

V1.1   10 March 2015
       Added option to mute warning alarms and full alarms separately, and
       Added warning and alarm beep test menu options to check volume levels 
       on device.

V1.0   07 March 2015
       First Play Store release
